How we designed and created our brochure?

Given that we all have a very busy lifestyle, it often happens that we just want a quick overview about a subject. One of the fastest ways people find information about things that they do not know, is reading a brochure. Our design of brochure has the intention to inform our plan of action by sharing our motivation for a project and the reasons for its implementation. Several basic sections were established : the introduction, background, strategy and outcome.

The project is clearly explained so readers can folllow a logical progression of thought to the conclusion.

The final objective of the project is mentioned with a detail description of the tools we will use to achieve it.

Complete, Accurate Information in six different languages

During our first transnational meeting in Greece , we have chosen our logo style and decided that our brochure should communicate our intention with short sections broken up with a big headline. Each partner organisation has introduced a brief description profile to show our complementary aspects for the project. We made sure that in our brochure people can find things like: organisations names, logos and web addresses.

Once the brochure written in English was revised and approved by each partner, we continued to provide an accurate translation of the brochures in Turc, Hungarian, Croatian, French, Spanish and Greek in order to be distributed in paper version and online .
